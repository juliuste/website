Bahn-Guru

Deutsche Bahn price calendar. Wanna travel from A to B without spending all the money you earned by following those shady e-mails advising you to invest in nigerian cow medicine? Bahn-Guru will show you the cheapest DB tickets for each day in the next few weeks, so you know exactly when it's the best time to grab your stuff and take the next train.
